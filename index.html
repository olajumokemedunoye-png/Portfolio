💰 Financial Services
HR Attrition Analysis & Cost-of-Turnover Model
🗄️ SQL 📊 Excel ⚡ Power BI
🔍 Business Problem

Organisation experiencing 22% annual attrition with no visibility into where exits concentrated or what they cost. HR reporting was reactive and anecdotal — leadership had no financial picture of turnover risk.

⚙️ Approach

Built a multi-dimensional attrition model segmenting workforce by tenure bands, department, and performance ratings. Applied cost-of-turnover formula (recruitment + onboarding + ramp time) by role grade. SQL queries automated monthly headcount extraction from HRIS.

💡 Key Insights
68% of exits occurred within the first 18 months — pointing to onboarding failure, not compensation
Sales department had the highest attrition rate at 31% — 2x the company average
Employees working overtime showed significantly higher turnover risk across all departments
Mid-senior analyst exits cost an estimated £18,500 each in total replacement cost

🏥 Care & Healthcare
Staffing Ratio, Burnout Risk & Absence Forecasting
📊 Excel ⚡ Power BI 🗄️ SQL
🔍 Business Problem

Care provider facing 34% YoY rise in reactive agency spend. No early-warning system for burnout. Finance had no forecasting model to manage agency costs proactively.

⚙️ Approach

Built staffing load and absence trend model integrating Bradford Factor scores, shift patterns, and patient volume forecasts. Power BI flagged individuals exceeding burnout risk thresholds. Produced 6-week forward staffing forecasts.

💡 Key Insights
Monday and Friday shifts carried 3x the absence risk of mid-week — invisible in prior reporting
Night shift workers in 2–3 year tenure band showed highest burnout indicators
Top 20% of high-risk individuals accounted for 65% of all unplanned absence days
Rota redesign recommendation projected to reduce agency dependency by 18%

📞 Customer Operations
Customer Operations KPI & SLA Performance Dashboard
🗄️ PostgreSQL ⚡ Power BI 📋 Jira 📊 Excel
🔍 Business Problem

12,000+ monthly tickets managed across 3 disconnected systems. Manual reporting took 2–3 days per cycle. SLA breaches discovered after the fact with no root cause analysis.

⚙️ Approach

Designed unified KPI architecture across 14 metrics in 4 dimensions: Speed, Quality, Compliance, Productivity. PostgreSQL automated extraction from CRM and Jira. Power BI refreshed daily with drill-through to agent level.

💡 Key Insights
Top 3 complaint drivers — billing errors, delivery delays, account access — caused 74% of all SLA breaches
These were systemic process failures, not agent skill gaps — changing routing reduced repeat contacts by 28%
CSAT improved by 11 points in the quarter following specialist team routing implementation
Reporting cycle reduced from 3 days to 4 hours — a 70% efficiency gain
